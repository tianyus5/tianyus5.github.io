About

Currently I am a PhD candidate in the Ertekin group at the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. I am interested in using multiscale modeling to study the mechanism behind thrilling experimental findings or the intriguing properties of structural and functional materials.

Research

The simulation methods I use include density functional theory, cluster expansion, machine-learned interatomic potential, Monte Carlo, and molecular dynamics.
For the cluster expansion framework combining both magnetic and configurational degrees of freedom, the code is available now on GitHub.
